Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions about event photography, answered with insights from years of experience capturing NYC's most memorable moments.

Recently, a client asked me about my photography style. It's a question I get often, and honestly, I love talking about it. After spending years in the serenity of the darkroom, I've developed what I like to call a class film-noir look, documentary-style. It's about capturing those unscripted moments when people are just being themselves.

My approach is candid and editorial, creating elevated photographs that feel both timeless and artful. Whether it's a corporate event in Midtown or an intimate gathering in Brooklyn, I'm looking for those genuine moments - the real laugh between old friends, the quiet smile during a speech, the spontaneous dance moves when someone's favorite song comes on.
